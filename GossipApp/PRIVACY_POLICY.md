# Privacy Policy for GossipIn

**Last Updated:** October 10, 2025

## Introduction

GossipIn ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Email Address**: Used for account creation and authentication
- **Display Name**: Used to identify you within the app
- **User ID**: Automatically generated unique identifier

### Group Information
- **Group Names and Descriptions**: Information about groups you create or join
- **Messages**: Text messages you send in groups
- **Group Membership**: List of groups you belong to and your role (Admin, Approver, Member)

### Media and Files
- **Photos and Videos**: When you choose to share them in chats
- **Voice Messages**: When you record and send them
- **Documents**: When you attach and share them

### Communication Data
- **Call Logs**: Records of voice and video calls (duration, participants)
- **Message Timestamps**: When messages are sent
- **Group Activity**: Member approvals, role assignments

## Permissions We Request

### Required Permissions

**INTERNET**
- Purpose: Enable messaging, calling, and data synchronization
- Required: Yes

**CAMERA**
- Purpose: Take photos/videos to share in chats, video calling
- Required: Only when using camera features
- User Control: You can deny this permission and still use text messaging

**READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_MEDIA_VIDEO / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE**
- Purpose: Access photos and videos you want to share
- Required: Only when sharing media
- User Control: Can be denied; affects only media sharing

**RECORD_AUDIO / MICROPHONE**
- Purpose: Voice messages and voice/video calling
- Required: Only when using voice features
- User Control: Can be denied; affects only audio features

**ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE**
- Purpose: Check internet connectivity
- Required: Yes for core functionality

**VIBRATE**
- Purpose: Notification feedback (future feature)
- Required: No

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
1. **Authentication**: Verify your identity and maintain your session
2. **Messaging**: Deliver messages to group members
3. **Group Management**: Display groups, members, and roles
4. **Communication**: Enable voice/video calls between users
5. **Media Sharing**: Store and display shared files temporarily

### We DO NOT:
- ❌ Sell your personal information to third parties
- ❌ Share your data with advertisers
- ❌ Track your location
- ❌ Access your contacts without permission
- ❌ Read your other apps' data
- ❌ Use your information for marketing without consent

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
- **Current Version**: All data is stored locally on your device
- **Session Data**: Groups, messages, and user info persist during app usage
- **No Cloud Sync**: Currently, data is not backed up to cloud servers

### Future Updates
- We may introduce optional cloud backup with Firebase
- You will be notified and can opt-in
- End-to-end encryption will be implemented

## Data Sharing

### We Share Data Only When:
1. **Required by Law**: If legally obligated to disclose information
2. **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly agree to share
3. **Service Providers**: Future third-party services (with notice)

### We Never Share:
- Your messages with other apps
- Your personal information with advertisers
- Your group membership with non-members
- Your files with unauthorized parties

## Your Rights

### You Can:
1. **Access Your Data**: View all information stored
2. **Delete Your Account**: Request account deletion (data removed)
3. **Export Your Data**: Request a copy of your information
4. **Control Permissions**: Enable/disable camera, microphone, storage
5. **Leave Groups**: Exit any group at any time
6. **Delete Messages**: Remove your sent messages (future feature)

### How to Exercise Rights:
- Email us at: privacy@gossipin.app (or your support email)
- Use in-app settings (future feature)
- Delete the app to remove local data

## Children's Privacy

GossipIn is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Security

### Our Security Measures:
- **Authentication**: Secure login with password validation
- **Local Storage**: Data stored securely on device
- **No Plaintext Passwords**: Passwords are not stored in readable format
- **Encrypted Calls**: Indicator shown during calls
- **Role-Based Access**: Permission controls prevent unauthorized access

### Your Responsibility:
- Keep your password secure
- Logout on shared devices
- Don't share your account credentials
- Be cautious with group invite codes

## Third-Party Services

### Current Version:
- No third-party analytics
- No advertising networks
- No social media integrations
- No external data processors

### Future Services (With Notice):
- Firebase (authentication, database, storage)
- Cloud messaging (push notifications)
- Analytics (app improvement)

You will be notified before any third-party integration.

## Group Privacy

### Group Creators/Admins Can:
- Set group privacy (Public/Private)
- Add/remove members
- Approve/reject join requests
- Assign roles (Admin/Approver/Member)
- View all group messages
- Delete groups

### Members Should Know:
- Admins can see all group activity
- Messages are visible to all approved group members
- Private groups are invite-only
- Public groups can be discovered
- Terms & conditions may apply per group

## Data Retention

### During Active Use:

### Upon Account Deletion:

- Current version: No automatic deletion (local storage only)
