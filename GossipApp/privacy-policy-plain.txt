1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to GossipIn ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our mobile application.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Personal Information
We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:
- Register for an account (email address, username)
- Create or join a group
- Send messages or communicate with other users
- Upload photos, videos, or other content

2.2 Automatically Collected Information
When you use our app, we may automatically collect:
- Device information (model, operating system, unique device identifiers)
- Usage data (features used, time spent, interactions)
- Log data (IP address, access times, app crashes)

3. PERMISSIONS WE REQUEST

3.1 CAMERA Permission (android.permission.CAMERA)
- WHY: To allow you to take photos and videos for sharing in group chats
- WHEN: Only requested when you tap camera/photo features
- OPTIONAL: You can use the app without granting camera access
- DATA STORAGE: Photos/videos are stored locally and optionally uploaded to your groups

3.2 MICROPHONE Permission (android.permission.RECORD_AUDIO)
- WHY: For voice messages and voice/video calling features
- WHEN: Only requested when you use voice message or calling features
- OPTIONAL: Text chat works without microphone access
- DATA STORAGE: Voice messages are transmitted securely

3.3 STORAGE Permission (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, READ_MEDIA_IMAGES, READ_MEDIA_VIDEO)
- WHY: To save and access photos, videos, and documents you want to share
- WHEN: When you select media to share or save received media
- OPTIONAL: You can use basic chat features without storage access
- DATA STORAGE: Media is stored locally on your device

3.4 INTERNET Permission (android.permission.INTERNET)
- WHY: Required for all app functionality (messaging, authentication, sync)
- WHEN: Always active when using the app
- REQUIRED: Essential for the app to function
- DATA USAGE: Transmits messages, media, and app data

3.5 NETWORK STATE Permission (android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE)
- WHY: To detect internet connectivity and optimize performance
- WHEN: Always active
- REQUIRED: Ensures smooth app experience
- DATA USAGE: Checks connection status only
