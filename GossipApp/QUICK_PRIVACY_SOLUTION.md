# 🚀 QUICK Privacy Policy Solution (2 Minutes)

## ✅ **Fastest Way to Get Privacy Policy URL:**

### **Option 1: GitHub Gist (Recommended - 2 Minutes)**

#### **Step-by-Step:**

1. **Go to:** https://gist.github.com

2. **Create New Gist:**
   - Click green "+ Create new gist" button

3. **Add Content:**
   - Filename: `privacy-policy.html`
   - Description: `GossipIn Privacy Policy`
   - Copy ENTIRE content from: `privacy-policy.html`
   - Paste into gist

4. **Create Public Gist:**
   - Click "Create public gist" button

5. **Get URL:**
   - Click "Raw" button (top right)
   - Copy the URL
   - Format: `https://gist.githubusercontent.com/[user]/[id]/raw/[hash]/privacy-policy.html`

6. **Use in Play Console:**
   - App Content → Privacy Policy
   - Paste URL
   - Save ✅

**Time:** 2 minutes  
**Cost:** FREE  
**Works Forever:** ✅  
**HTTPS:** ✅  
**Play Store Approved:** ✅
