🎉 GossipIn Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 10, 2025

Introduction

GossipIn ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email Address: Used for account creation and authentication
  • Display Name: Used to identify you within the app
  • User ID: Automatically generated unique identifier

Group Information

  • Group Names and Descriptions: Information about groups you create or join
  • Messages: Text messages you send in groups
  • Group Membership: List of groups you belong to and your role

Media and Files

  • Photos and Videos: When you choose to share them in chats
  • Voice Messages: When you record and send them
  • Documents: When you attach and share them

Permissions Explained

📷 CAMERA Permission

Why we need it: To allow you to take photos/videos for sharing and enable video calling features.

When it's used: Only when you actively choose to use the camera.

Can you refuse? Yes. You can still use all text messaging features without granting camera access.

🎤 MICROPHONE Permission

Why we need it: For voice messages and voice/video calling.

When it's used: Only during calls or when recording voice messages.

Can you refuse? Yes. Text messaging and other features will still work.

📁 STORAGE Permission

Why we need it: To access photos/videos you want to share.

When it's used: Only when you select media to share.

Can you refuse? Yes. You can still use text chat and other features.

🌐 INTERNET Permission

Why we need it: Core app functionality for messaging and calls.

When it's used: Throughout app usage.

Can you refuse? No. Required for the app to function.

How We Use Your Information

Primary Uses

  1. Authentication: Verify your identity and maintain your session
  2. Messaging: Deliver messages to group members
  3. Group Management: Display groups, members, and roles
  4. Communication: Enable voice/video calls between users
  5. Media Sharing: Store and display shared files

✅ What We DON'T Do

  • We don't sell your personal information
  • We don't share your data with advertisers
  • We don't track your location
  • We don't access your contacts without permission
  • We don't read your other apps' data

Data Storage and Security

Current Implementation

  • Local Storage: All data stored on your device
  • No Cloud Backup: Data not uploaded to servers (currently)
  • Session-Based: Data persists during app usage

Security Measures

  • Secure authentication with password validation
  • Role-based access controls
  • Encrypted call indicator
  • Protected local storage

Your Rights and Control

You Can Always:

  • View all your data within the app
  • Delete your account and data
  • Leave any group
  • Control app permissions in device settings
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data

Third-Party Services

Current Version: No third-party services are integrated.

Future Updates: We may integrate Firebase, analytics, or push notifications. You will be notified before implementation.

Children's Privacy

GossipIn is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be communicated through the app or via email.

Data Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Contact us at privacy@gossipin.app
  2. Or uninstall the app (removes all local data)
  3. Cloud data (if any) will be deleted within 30 days

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy:

Email: privacy@gossipin.app

App: GossipIn - Secure Messaging

Response Time: Within 48 hours

⚠️ Important Note

This privacy policy applies to the GossipIn mobile application. By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.
